🏆 What Is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a national STEM competition where student teams compete in events covering biology, earth science, chemistry, physics, technology, and engineering. Virginia Association of Science Olympiad (VASO) runs the Division A program for elementary students in Grades 3–4.

📅 2025–2026 VA Division A Events

  • Build events (45 min): Tower Building (3rd grade), Bungee Egg Drop, Astronaut Lander, Bridge Building, Ping Pong Parachute, Gravity Vehicle, and more
  • Written events (30 min): Oceans (3rd grade), Anatomy, Data Investigations, Extremophiles, Solar System, and more
  • Tournaments: Blueprint, Infrared, Chlorophyll, Dark Matter — held at Oakton HS or Lake Braddock

📦 How to Use This Portal

  • Pick your project from the cards above — each has its own full page
  • Read the official instructions first — rules, materials, and exact scoring from the VA manual
  • Study the slides — at least twice before competition day
  • Use the shopping list to buy practice materials from Walmart
  • Take practice tests and review wrong answers before competition
  • Bring required safety glasses — no glasses = no-show score

⚠️ Safety Requirements

  • Tower Building + Astronaut Lander: ANSI Z87+ impact-rated safety glasses. Must be stamped "Z87+" on the lens. Eyeglasses do not count.
  • Bungee Egg Drop: ANSI Z87 D3 splash/droplet-rated goggles (full seal). Must show "D3" rating. Regular glasses or impact-only glasses do NOT qualify.
  • All team members must wear eye protection during any competitor's testing phase — not just their own.
  • Safety gear is not provided at the event. Buy before competition day.

📋 Official Scoring Overview — All Build Events

🏗️ Tower Building
FS = Assessment (50 pts) + Height (50 pts)
Tiebreakers: time to failure → unused straws → assessment score
🥚 Bungee Egg Drop
FS = Assessment (50 pts) + Best Drop Score (50 pts)
Closer to Artificial Horizon = higher drop score
🚀 Astronaut Lander
FS = Assessment (50 pts) + Flight Time (50 pts)
Marshmallow exits cup = Tier 2 (−20%)
